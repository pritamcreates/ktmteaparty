Who We Are

A community that moves together

KTM Tea Party is Kathmandu's home for high-energy, high-fidelity experiences. We bring together the city's most curious, expressive, and alive people — one event at a time.

Every month we curate a space where music, connection, and culture collide. Not just a party. A ritual. Come for the vibe, stay for the community.
